**1. AI Impact Summit 2026**

Scheduled from February 16 to 20, 2026, in New Delhi, India, the AI Impact Summit aims to focus on the practical impact, implementation, and measurable outcomes of AI technologies. Waymo has introduced the Waymo World Model, a new frontier for autonomous driving simulation. Built on Google DeepMind’s Genie 3 world model, this tool can create highly realistic and diverse driving scenes, including rare and extreme scenarios, enabling comprehensive testing of autonomous vehicle behavior. Utah has partnered with health tech startup Doctronic to launch an AI system that autonomously renews approximately 200 common medications after performing clinical evaluations. **6. AI in Scientific Research**

Researchers have developed a methodology for evaluating AI models as scientific research assistants, known as EAIRA. This approach includes multiple-choice questions, open responses, lab-style experiments, and field-style experiments to assess AI's effectiveness in real-world scientific applications. ([arxiv.org](https://arxiv.org/abs/2502.20309?utm_source=openai))

**7. AI in Forecasting**

A new system has been introduced to automate the generation and resolution of high-quality forecasting questions using large language model-powered web research agents. This system has produced 1,499 diverse, real-world forecasting questions, demonstrating the potential of AI in enhancing forecasting accuracy. ([arxiv.org](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.22444?utm_source=openai))
